Our perfume watchlist

Over the years we have experienced a major uprising in the female Gucci scents and there is no suprise as they are highly succesful in whatever they set out to be. Therefore, here at Afterfume we are putting Gucci's Bloom as top priority.


Prada Paradoxe Intense manages to keep coming back into our watchlists. There is something about this scent that Prada nailed and it is definitley one for the rotation.


Black Orchid from Tom Ford is a fragrance that you ask everyone about and they have heard. There is a reason why the fragrance is chart-toppping in the past and im sure it'll climb it's way back.


Carolina Herrera with Good Girl scrapes in by the narrowest of margins however, well deserved nonethless as this perfume is a go getter for the summer months that are soon to be upon us.
